# FINDR HEALTH • BRAND IDENTITY GUIDELINES

**Project: FINDR Visual System Expansion**

---

### **Slide 1: Introduction and Brand Story**
**The New Standard in Health Advocacy**
FINDR Health was established to solve the systemic opacity of medical billing. At its core, the brand represents a "Guardian" persona—a sophisticated, AI-driven entity that provides clarity where there was once confusion. 

**Brand Context:**
The identity is built upon the dual pillar of **Precision** and **Empathy**. Our visual language must communicate the technical excellence of a high-frequency trading firm while maintaining the approachability of a trusted family advocate. Every pixel in this system is designed to reassure the user that their financial health is in the most capable hands.

---

### **Slide 2: The Emblem/Logo Architecture**
**Logo Geometry and Synthesis**
The FINDR emblem is a combined mark consisting of the "F-Sentinel" symbol and the custom "Findr" wordmark. The relationship between these elements is defined by a rigorous grid system.

*   **Distinctive Element:** The "F-Sentinel" (The first character), which acts as a shield and an auditor.
*   **Wordmark:** A custom-weighted sans-serif that provides human balance to the technical symbol.
*   **Secondary Symbol:** None. The mark must always be used in its primary or "F" solo form for small-scale branding.

**Technical Description:**
The geometry follows a 4:3 aspect ratio for the overall mark, with the vertical stem of the "F" serving as the baseline for all subsequent letterforms.

---

### **Slide 3: Clear Space & Visual Isolation**
**Defining the Exclusion Zone**
To maintain the prestige and legibility of the FINDR identity, a precise "Exclusion Zone" has been established. This area must be kept free of any competing visual elements, including typography, photography, or other logos.

*   **Unit of Measure:** Use the height of the "i" in "Findr" (denoted as "X").
*   **Clear Space Rule:** A minimum of **1x** must be maintained around the entire perimeter of the logo. 
*   **Minimum Size:**
    *   **Digital:** 32px height for primary mark.
    *   **Print:** 8mm height for trademark inclusion.

### **Slide 5: Coordination with Backgrounds**
**Visibility and Contrast Protocols**
The logo's visibility is paramount. It must behave according to the following environmental rules:

*   **Light Backgrounds:** Primary Blue logo preferred.
*   **Dark Backgrounds:** Solid White logo only. Never use Zest or Blue on dark surfaces without high-contrast backing.
*   **Photographic Backgrounds:** The logo must be placed in a "low-energy" area of the image. Standard blur application (20px+) is required if the image complexity is high.
*   **Gradients:** When placed on the "Mesh Mesh" gradient, the logo should be pure Black or pure White depending on the dominant mesh color.

---

### **Slide 6: Incorrect Usage (The "Don’ts")**
**Maintenance of Integrity**
The following actions are strictly prohibited and result in immediate brand dilution:
1.  **Do Not Stretch:** No non-proportional scaling is allowed.
2.  **No Color Swapping:** The "F" symbol must not be a different color from the wordmark within the same instance.
3.  **No Outlines:** The logo should inhabit a solid state; never use outlines to "fix" poor placement.
4.  **No Shadowing:** Standard CSS drop shadows are forbidden on the logo itself.
