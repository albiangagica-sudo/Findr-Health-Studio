# FINDR Health Brand Guidelines
*Version 1.0 (2026)*

***

## Slide 1: Introduction & Brand Story
**The Health Guardian Persona**

FINDR is not just a technology platform; it is a clinical advocate for the modern consumer. Born from the need to navigate the labyrinthine complexities of the healthcare financial system, FINDR serves as a "Guardian" for the patient. 

Our mission is to translate medical complexity into financial clarity. We believe that every individual deserves to understand their healthcare costs without fear or confusion. Through technical transparency and empathetic design, we empower our users to take control of their medical journeys.

***

## Slide 2: Logo Architecture & Geometry
**The Distinctive Symbol & Wordmark**

The FINDR Emblem is a geometric representation of connectivity and protection. 

1. **The Distinctive Element**: The stylized "f" (represented by the first path) is designed with a broad, protective curve, symbolizing the umbrella of advocacy.
2. **The Geometry**: The icon consists of five distinct path segments that create a harmonious flow from left to right.
3. **The Relationship**: The "i", "n", "d", and "r" elements (the secondary symbols) maintain consistent vertical alignment and negative space, ensuring the brand identity remains legible and authoritative across all displays.

***

## Slide 3: Clear Space & Minimum Sizes
**Ensuring Visual Integrity**

To maintain the authority of the FINDR brand, the logo must always breathe.

1. **The Exclusion Zone**: A clear space equivalent to the height of the "dot" in the "i" element (the square in the 'g' group) must be maintained around all four sides of the emblem. No text, imagery, or UI elements should enter this zone.
2. **Minimum Size**: 
   - **Digital**: 120px wide (to ensure the "i" detail is visible).
   - **Print**: 30mm width for standard business collateral.

***

## Slide 4: Display Colors
**Technical Specifications**

The FINDR palette is designed for high contrast and readability, mirroring the "Liquid Glassmorphism" aesthetic.

- **Primary Blue (Findr Blue / Cobalt)**: 
  - HEX: `#2E5BFF`
  - RGB: `46, 91, 255`
  - CMYK: `82, 64, 0, 0`
- **Secondary Green (Zest)**:
  - HEX: `#D4FF59`
  - RGB: `212, 255, 89`
- **Lavander (Accent)**:
  - HEX: `#EED9FF`
- **Surface (Teal Accent)**:
  - HEX: `#17DDC0` (Default Logo Fill)

***

## Slide 5: Coordination with Backgrounds
**Visibility Rules**

- **Light Backgrounds**: Use the Primary Blue or Teal versions of the logo.
- **Dark Backgrounds**: The logo should be rendered in Zest Green or White.
- **Photographic Backgrounds**: The "Liquid Glass" effect must be applied via a backdrop-blur container behind the logo to ensure the 4.5:1 accessibility contrast ratio is met.

***

## Slide 6: Incorrect Usage
**The Prohibitions**

To preserve the prestige of the organization, the following are strictly forbidden:
- **No Stretching**: Never alter the aspect ratio of the SVG.
- **No Shadowing**: Do not add drop shadows to the logo unless it is part of a Glassmorphism card.
- **No Separation**: The five elements of the emblem must never be rearranged or presented in isolation.

***

## Slide 7: Typography Hierarchy
**Text Fonts for Advocacy**

- **Primary Typeface**: **Inter** (Sans-Serif). Used for all body copy and user interfaces to ensure authoritative readability.
- **Secondary/Display**: **Space Grotesk**. Used for hero headings and technical data points, providing a tech-forward, distinctive feel.

***

## Slide 8: Application Examples
**FINDR in the Real World**

- **Stationery**: Letterheads should use `#2E5BFF` headers with 12pt Inter body text.
- **Digital Interface**: The navigation bar must feature the Teal (`#17DDC0`) logo on the left, with the sentinel gate protecting all internal dashboard routes.
- **News Releases**: Official announcements must lead with the "Introduction & Brand Story" to establish the Health Guardian tone.
